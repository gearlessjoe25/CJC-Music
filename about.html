About CJC

Meet the minds behind the music.

Cheyne Jackson

Chxyne

Cheyne is the voice behind most the song. He grew up in school choir and band with Joseph from middle school through high school. Although he put a halt on his music endeavors, he picked up a mic again towards the end of College and has been making music ever since.

@imchxyne

Joseph Buadoo

Joë

Joseph also known as JJ is one of the talents behind the instrumentals and beats. He grew up with Cheyne and was very music orientated, playing the drums at church, saxophone at school, and singing in choir in school. After a dull rough patch in in life, the motivation from Chris and support from Cheyne got JJ on the fast producing track.

@gearlessjoe_25

Chrisopher Heyward

C-Xarp

Chris is the other side of the instrumentals and beats with his sampling prowess. Back in the day, he use to produce and release beats on SoundCloud, but life got in the way and halted that hobby. After a long hiatus, he hopped on Ableton and grew his skills along side JJ.

@realghrobot
